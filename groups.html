Our Church Groups

Catholic Men Association (CMA)

The Catholic Men Association brings together men of all ages to grow in faith, serve the community, and support one another in living out Catholic values. We meet every Saturday morning for prayer, fellowship, and discussions on applying our faith in daily life.

Meeting Time: Saturdays, 8:00 AM

Contact: John Mwangi - 0722 123 456

Catholic Women Association (CWA)

The Catholic Women Association is a vibrant group dedicated to spiritual growth, charitable works, and supporting the church and community. Our activities include prayer meetings, fundraising for church projects, and visiting the sick.

Meeting Time: Wednesdays, 2:00 PM

Contact: Mary Wanjiku - 0723 456 789

Young Catholic Association (YCA)

For young adults aged 18-35, the YCA provides a space to explore faith, build relationships, and serve the community. We organize retreats, social events, and volunteer opportunities while deepening our understanding of Catholic teachings.

Meeting Time: Fridays, 5:30 PM

Contact: Paul Kimani - 0711 987 654

Youth Serving Christ (YSC)

Our youth group for teens (13-18 years) focuses on faith formation through dynamic discussions, music, and service projects. We help young people navigate modern challenges while staying true to Catholic values.

Meeting Time: Sundays after Mass

Contact: Sr. Grace - 0720 111 222

Parish Missionary Childhood (PMC)

The PMC helps children (6-12 years) learn about their faith in fun and engaging ways while developing a missionary spirit. Through games, songs, and activities, we nurture the seeds of faith in our youngest parishioners.

Meeting Time: Sundays, 10:00 AM

Contact: Teacher Ann - 0733 555 777

Legion of Mary

The Legion of Mary is a worldwide apostolic organization of lay Catholics who, with the sanction of the Church and under the powerful leadership of Mary Immaculate, serve the Church and their neighbor on a voluntary basis.

Meeting Time: Tuesdays, 4:00 PM

Contact: Joseph Kamau - 0724 888 999
