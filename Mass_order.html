Mass Order Schedule

Join us in celebrating the Holy Eucharist according to the following schedule. All are welcome to participate in our liturgical celebrations.

Regular Mass Schedule

  • Sunday Masses:
    • 7:00 AM (Kiswahili)
    • 9:00 AM (English)
    • 11:00 AM (Kikuyu)
  • Weekday Masses: Monday – Friday at 6:30 AM and 5:30 PM
  • Saturday Mass: 6:30 PM (Vigil Mass)

Special Devotions

  • First Friday: Devotion Mass at 7:00 PM followed by Eucharistic Adoration
  • First Saturday: Marian Devotion at 8:00 AM
  • Rosary: Daily before morning Mass (6:00 AM) and evening Mass (5:00 PM)

Sacrament of Reconciliation

  • Confessions: Saturdays 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM or by appointment
  • Before Mass: Available 30 minutes before each Mass upon request

For special Mass intentions or to schedule a private Mass, please contact the parish office during working hours or speak with one of the priests after Mass.
